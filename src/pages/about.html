About Vallabh Prefab

Our Mission

We deliver durable, fast-deploying and customizable prefabricated structures manufactured using modern industrial techniques — supporting builders, developers and institutions to accelerate projects while maintaining high quality.

Manufacturing & Engineering

Our factory uses standardized production lines and quality checkpoints to produce steel-framed modules, cement-board panels and insulated systems. Each unit undergoes dimensional checks, finish inspections and pre-installation testing.

  • Steel frame welding & anti-corrosion treatments
  • Precision panel fabrication
  • Insulation & weatherproofing options (PU foam, rockwool)
  • Pre-fitted plumbing and electrical cutouts

Sustainability & Quality

Factory production reduces on-site waste and emissions. We source durable materials and follow documented quality and safety processes. Certifications and compliance are available on request.

Vision & Capabilities

We aim to scale modular manufacturing to serve large commercial and government programs — offering turnkey prefabrication, transport logistics, and on-site installation teams.
